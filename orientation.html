IITs — Technology

Indian Institutes of Technology
⏱ ~10 min
23IITs in India
~17KJEE Advanced seats
11.5L+JEE Main aspirants
₹20L+Avg. CTC (top IITs)
  • History: IIT Kharagpur established 1951 — first of its kind in India
  • Admission via JEE Main → JEE Advanced; only ~2.5% of Main qualifiers make it
  • Old IITs: Bombay, Delhi, Madras, Kanpur, Kharagpur, Roorkee, Guwahati
  • Notable alumni: Sundar Pichai (Google), Satya Nadella went to IIT Manipal, N.R. Narayana Murthy (IIT IISc)
  • Research & startups: IITs filed 2,800+ patents in 2023

IIMs — Management

Indian Institutes of Management
⏱ ~10 min
21IIMs in India
3.5L+CAT aspirants/year
~4,000PGP seats (top 6)
₹35L+Avg. CTC (IIM-A)
  • Flagships: IIM Ahmedabad (1961), IIM Bangalore (1973), IIM Calcutta (1961)
  • CAT (Common Admission Test): 99+ percentile needed for top IIMs
  • Selection: CAT → WAT + PI rounds; work experience valued
  • Global MBA rankings: IIM-A, B, C consistently in FT Global top 50
  • PGP (2-yr MBA) vs PGPX (1-yr executive): different tracks for fresh vs experienced

05

Other Premier Institutes

NLUs, NITs, IISc, TISS & more
⏱ ~10 min
  • NLUs: 25 National Law Universities via CLAT; NLSIU Bangalore & NLU Delhi are flagships
  • NITs: 31 NITs across India; admission via JEE Main (state + all-India quota)
  • IISc Bangalore: India's top research university; QS rank #186 globally (2024)
  • IISERs: 7 institutes for BS-MS in pure sciences; admission via KVPY/JEE/SAT
  • TISS: Tata Institute of Social Sciences — premier for social work & HR
  • XLRI, FMS, SRCC: Top choices for management & commerce outside IIMs

06

How to Prepare

Roadmap for aspirants
⏱ ~10 min + Q&A
  • Start early: Class 9–10 is ideal for foundation building in science & math
  • Coaching vs self-study: NCERT mastery first, then advanced material
  • Mock tests: attempt full-length mocks from Class 11 onwards
  • Time management: balance board exams + entrance prep simultaneously
  • Q&A / open discussion: address specific doubts & stream choices
